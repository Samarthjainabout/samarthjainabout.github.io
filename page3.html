Avalanch breakdown in BLDC controller mosfets

When the motor power supply of controller suddenly falls from 50V to 24V,Then due to sudden change in voltage avalanch occurs in the mosfets and causes them to break apart

During avalanch the energy produced should be below 13 mJ for IRF540N(from the datasheet).From the above graph and below eq-1 we have avalanch energy (assuming motor inductance to be 3mH and current to be 5Amps measured during experiment)=(70/(70-25))*.5*3mH*25(amps)^2.
This equals to 58mJouls, which is bigger than13mJouls as ber datasheet.Hence the mosfet avalanch
